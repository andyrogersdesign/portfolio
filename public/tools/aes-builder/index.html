AES Survey Builder

Build an Actionable Experience Survey and share it as a link — respondents open the link, take the survey in their browser, and send you their responses. Nothing goes through a server; everything runs locally on your machine and theirs.

How this works

Fill in the form below and click Copy shareable link. The link contains your whole survey encoded in the URL — there’s no server or database. Send it to your respondents. When they finish, they can email their responses back to you, or copy a response link for you to paste into the AES report analyser.

For an LMS deployment, use the SCORM package instead — same method, different delivery.
